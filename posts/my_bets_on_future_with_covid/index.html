My Bets on Future with Covid

2020-11-17
covid , bets

I’m no medical professional nor do I have any prior personal experience with COVID19 or any related pandemic. The last serious one in my country was Swine Flu and I was in my teens at that time.

But allow me to make some predictions, Mark My Words style.

Predictions

  • At least 3 types of vaccines will be developed and used worldwide and problems will be found in all of them
  • The vaccines will be known to help only certain people (say a good 95% of them) but only for a short while. This combination of a contagious virus and a not so effective vaccine will make sure that people still wear masks for a long time even with vaccines developed
  • We might develop drugs that treat the symptoms of the virus rather than the virus itself so we might learn how to live with COVID just like we live with common flu
  • Since we know that most people aren’t that affected by this virus, I predict that some genetic (or some other) pattern will be found and this will be leveraged in treatment or subsequent vaccines

I’ll edit this post in a year to see how far this it is removed from reality. I hope I’m well enough to be able to do this at that time though!
